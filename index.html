Aarav Bhuta — Forward

Playing football since the age of 5, Aarav has represented his school in inter-school tournaments, district level competitions, and international tournaments. A clinical finisher with elite-level ambitions, he aspires to be selected for national level prestigious clubs and compete in the ISL.

After training camps with the Steven Gerrard Academy in Liverpool (2023 & 2025) and FCV in Loughborough (2024), Aarav proved himself at the U21 level — scoring 4 goals and 3 assists in 5 games during the SGA 2025 camp.
